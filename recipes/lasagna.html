Lasagna

This is a very special recipe of mine as it is one of the first recipes that I learnt how to make before I went to university! It is also one that I had made for me multiple times by my mother, who in turn handed the recipe over to me

The recipe itself is quite basic but will produce a hearty, warming plate of italian pasta goodness!

Ingredients List

  • Lasagna sheets * 4
  • 800g jar of passata
  • 500ml White Sauce
  • 150g parmesan
  • Tomato puree
  • 700g beef mince
  • olive oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • Bouquet Garnee
