Lasagna

This is a very special recipe of mine as it is one of the first recipes that I learnt how to make before I went to university! It is also one that I had made for me multiple times by my mother, who in turn handed the recipe over to me

The recipe itself is quite basic but will produce a hearty, warming plate of italian pasta goodness!

Ingredients List

  • Lasagna sheets * 4
  • 800g jar of passata
  • 500ml White Sauce
  • 150g parmesan
  • Tomato puree
  • 700g beef mince
  • olive oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • Bouquet Garnee
  • 1 small bottle of red wine
  • Worcester Sauce

Instructions

  1. Add the oil to a pan and set to a low heat. Once the temperature is high enough, add the onion and fry slowly until translucent.
  2. Add mince to the pan, increase the heat and fry until brown.
  3. Add Passata, puree, Red wine, worcester sauce and bouquet garnee to the pan and bring to boil cook sauce for 20 minutes.
  4. While the sauce is boiling set the oven to 200 degrees and prepare the tray that the lasagna will be constructed in.
  5. Once the sauce has cooked. Construct the lasaga in the dish. The order being sauce -> pasta sheets -> white Sauce -> sauce -> pasta sheets. The last layer should be the mince sauce. Once full top off the dish with a layer of parmesan cheese.
  6. Place the dish in the oven and cook for 40 minutes
  7. There you have it, enough lasagna for 4 people ready to eat!
