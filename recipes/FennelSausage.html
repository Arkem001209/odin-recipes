Fennel Sausage Rigatoni Pasta

This is the big one. I cant take any credit for this as I discovered this one at my dear friend Lauras 30th birthday. Her good friend from South Africa (her home) made This plate of fennelly spicy sausagy goodness for us and my life hasnt been the same since. The sweetness of the fenel, richness from the cream and sausage and tang from the tomato and chilli flakes work to create the ultimate dinner time comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Sticks of Celery/1 Fennel
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Fennel Seeds
  • 2 Cups Double Cream
  • 150ml White Wine
  • 400g Sausage Meat
  • 500g Rigatoni Pasta
  • Minced Garlic
  • 200g Parmesan
  • 2 Tablespoons Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Fresh Parsely
